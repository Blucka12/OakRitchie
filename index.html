Welcome to my site!

Welcome to the central hub for my work over the years. I have been honored to work in the film industry; from short films and documentaries to major motion pictures, such as "Gods and Generals"; the music industry, as a professional rock percussionist; and the television business with over 100 episodes of web television content with guests of various expertise. I now am developing my skills as an information professional, with skills building in web development, systems analysis, data analysis, social media algorithmic analysis and data visualization, just to name a few. These skills all synthesize into a broad but focused repository of knowledge of a diverse experience of arts, science and social development.


Diversity

Be it theology, philosophy, philanthropy or artistry, I have dipped deeply into all of them. A consistent reflective process of the nature of the insistent self, or ego, also steers me clear of indulging in behaviors that result in a lack of modesty.
